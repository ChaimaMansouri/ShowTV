Alchemy of Souls is a popular South Korean fantasy drama that aired in 2022. Set in a mystical, fictional kingdom called Daeho, the story explores themes of magic, forbidden love, and destiny. The series revolves around mages who possess the ability to perform "soul shifting," a forbidden technique that allows souls to switch bodies, creating chaos in their world.
